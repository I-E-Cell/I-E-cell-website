The participants are requested to make their own teams of at least 2-3 members.

The teams will be divided into batches of two teams, each receiving a company with similar business interests to represent. (Example:- Uber and Ola).

Each team will be given 20 mins to prepare about the company/startup given to them before presenting on the stage.

These batches will be called upon the stage to present their cases together( or rather against each other). The two teams will be performing simultaneously on the stage.

Extra points are there for inter-branch teams.

Each team will be given a total of 3+1 minutes to explain the problem(s) they have identified in the case study and approaches to solve them. Post that the judges will ask the questions.

You can use a whiteboard to explain your approach, no ppt are allowed.

Once we are done with all 16 teams a 15-mins break will be taken to compile the scores and post the results will be announced.

All team members must be present.
